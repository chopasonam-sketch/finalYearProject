Message

Building confident, skilled and responsible professionals

Dear Students, Faculty and Staff,

It is a matter of great privilege for me to serve as the Principal of this institution. I am committed to upholding the values and traditions that have made our technical institute a center of excellence in education. Our mission is to provide you with the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

In this era of globalization, when there is a growing need for qualified professionals, Polytechnic Colleges play a vital role in shaping industry-ready individuals. Our institution provides modern infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and an environment that promotes both academic excellence and ethical values.

We strongly encourage students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and extracurricular activities. Participation in sports, cultural events, and innovation projects helps in building a well-rounded personality.

Academic Excellence Strengthening technical knowledge through focused learning.
Ethical Growth Encouraging discipline, responsibility, and teamwork.
Innovation Mindset Preparing students for practical industry challenges.

Success is not a destination but a continuous journey that demands perseverance, discipline, and dedication. I am confident that each one of you has the potential to achieve great heights. I wish you all a successful and rewarding academic year ahead.

Sajad Ahmed
Er. Sajad Ahmed Khan Principal, Govt. Polytechnic College Leh
