How to Reach

The college is located near State Bank of India, Choglamsar, close to the main road, making it easily accessible for students and visitors.

  • Located near State Bank of India, Choglamsar
  • Situated close to the main road for easy access
  • Nearby Landmark: Mentokling Residency, Ladakh
  • Well connected by local transport and taxis
  • Nearest Bus Stand: Leh Bus Stand
  • Nearest Airport: Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport, Leh

Students and visitors can easily reach the campus using local transport. It is advisable to check weather and road conditions, especially during winter.
