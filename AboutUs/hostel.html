Hostel Facilities

The college is in the process of developing separate hostel facilities for boys and girls to provide comfortable accommodation for students from different regions.

The hostel buildings are currently under construction and will be equipped with basic amenities to ensure a safe and convenient stay for students.

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls (under construction)
  • Planned rooms with essential facilities
  • Focus on safety and cleanliness
  • Peaceful environment for study and living

The hostel facilities will be available soon to support students who require on-campus accommodation.
