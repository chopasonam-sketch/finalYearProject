Mechanical Engineering Diploma

3-Year Diploma Program - Govt Polytechnic College Leh

About Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is one of the core branches of engineering that deals with machines, manufacturing, thermodynamics, automobile systems, and industrial production. It focuses on designing, analyzing, and maintaining mechanical systems.

Semester-wise Syllabus (3 Years)

Semester Main Subjects
1st Semester Engineering Mathematics, Engineering Physics, Workshop Practice, Engineering Drawing
2nd Semester Applied Mechanics, Engineering Chemistry, Basic Mechanical Engineering
3rd Semester Strength of Materials, Thermodynamics, Material Science
4th Semester Fluid Mechanics, Machine Drawing, Manufacturing Processes
5th Semester IC Engines, Refrigeration & Air Conditioning, Industrial Management
6th Semester Project Work, Industrial Training, Advanced Manufacturing Technology
