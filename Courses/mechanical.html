Mechanical Engineering

A hands-on diploma program covering machines, manufacturing, thermal systems, materials, maintenance, and industrial production.

About Department

Developing practical engineers for machines, workshops, and production systems

The Mechanical Engineering department helps students understand how machines are designed, built, operated, and maintained. The curriculum balances drawing, workshop practice, thermal science, manufacturing processes, and project work.
