Civil Engineering

About Department

Building the technical foundation for roads, structures, and public works

The Civil Engineering department trains students in construction materials, surveying, structural concepts, transportation engineering, and estimation. The program combines drawing, field work, and practical learning so students can contribute confidently on project sites.
