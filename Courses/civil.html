About Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is a core branch of engineering that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as roads, bridges, buildings, dams, and water systems. It is one of the oldest and most important engineering fields.

Semester-wise Syllabus (3 Years)

Semester Main Subjects
1st Semester Engineering Mathematics, Engineering Physics, Engineering Drawing, Workshop Practice
2nd Semester Civil Engineering Materials, Surveying Basics, Applied Chemistry
3rd Semester Strength of Materials, Surveying-I, Building Construction
4th Semester Surveying-II, Fluid Mechanics, RCC Structures
5th Semester Transportation Engineering, Estimation & Costing, Environmental Engineering
6th Semester Project Work, Industrial Training, Advanced Construction Techniques
