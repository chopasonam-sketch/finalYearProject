Civil Labs

The Civil Engineering department provides practical training to students in key areas such as construction materials, surveying, structural concepts, transportation engineering, and estimation.

The program combines engineering drawing, field work, and hands-on laboratory practice to help students gain real-world knowledge and technical skills required for construction and infrastructure projects.

  • Material Testing Lab for studying properties of construction materials
  • Surveying Lab for land measurement and field techniques
  • Structural Lab for understanding strength and design concepts
  • Transportation Engineering Lab for road and traffic studies
  • Practical exposure through field work and site visits

These laboratories help students develop confidence and skills to work effectively on construction sites and engineering projects.
