How admission will work for each session

The admission form is notified separately for each academic session. When the official notification is released, eligible students must fill the prescribed admission form and submit it to the respective Polytechnic where they want to take admission.

  1. 1
    Wait for the official notification The college/authority will notify the admission form and schedule for the concerned academic session.
  2. 2
    Fill the admission form Students must complete the form carefully with correct personal, academic, course, and contact details.
  3. 3
    Attach required documents Attach all required certificates and documents as mentioned in the admission notification.
  4. 4
    Submit at the respective Polytechnic The filled form should be submitted to the Polytechnic institution where the student wants to take admission.
  5. 5
    Follow further instructions After submission, students should follow the merit list, verification, fee, and reporting instructions issued by the concerned institution.
