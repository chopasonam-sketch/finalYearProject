Information for Students

The college provides important information and support to help students manage their academic journey smoothly and effectively.

  • Students must follow the academic calendar and attend classes regularly.
  • Minimum attendance is required to appear in examinations.
  • All students should maintain discipline and follow college rules.
  • Assignments, practicals, and projects must be submitted on time.
  • Students should regularly check notices and updates from the college.
  • Respect for faculty, staff, and fellow students is expected at all times.

Examinations

Internal tests and final examinations are conducted as per the schedule issued by the institution. Students are advised to prepare regularly and stay updated with exam notifications.

Support

Students can approach faculty members or the administration office for academic guidance, doubt clearing, or any support related to studies.
