Student Safety

Anti-Ragging Policy

Govt. Polytechnic College Leh maintains a zero-tolerance approach toward ragging and is committed to a safe, respectful, and welcoming campus environment for every student.

General Policy

Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited

Any act that causes physical harm, mental stress, fear, humiliation, harassment, or discomfort to a student is treated as ragging. Students are expected to maintain dignity, respect, and discipline in classrooms, hostels, labs, workshops, transport areas, and all campus spaces.

Acts Considered Ragging

Harassment or HumiliationTeasing, insulting, threatening, or forcing a student to do anything against their will.
Physical MisconductAny physical act that causes injury, fear, discomfort, or unsafe conditions.
Forced TasksCompelling juniors or classmates to perform personal work, odd tasks, or embarrassing acts.
Online MisuseBullying, threats, abusive messages, or sharing content meant to shame a student.
What To Do

Reporting Procedure

  • Step 1Do not ignore the incident. Move to a safe place and inform a trusted faculty member or class in-charge.
  • Step 2Report the matter to the Anti-Ragging Committee, HOD, Principal office, or grievance cell as soon as possible.
  • Step 3Provide basic details such as date, time, place, names if known, and any supporting evidence.
  • Step 4The college will review the complaint and take suitable action as per institutional rules.
Responsibilities

Every student has a role in keeping campus safe

For Senior StudentsWelcome juniors with respect, help them understand the campus, and avoid any behavior that may create fear.
For New StudentsSpeak up early, keep emergency contacts available, and report any uncomfortable situation without hesitation.
For WitnessesDo not encourage or record harmful behavior for entertainment. Support the affected student and report it.
Disciplinary ActionAny student found involved in ragging may face strict disciplinary action under applicable rules.
